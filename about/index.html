About Alberta Tech Solutions

Cybersecurity & IT Security Services in Edmonton

At Alberta Tech Solutions (ATS), we are committed to providing cutting-edge cybersecurity solutions and IT security services to protect businesses and homeowners alike. Based in Edmonton, our team of experts specializes in delivering reliable and innovative technology services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. From smart home automation to robust enterprise IT security, we offer a wide range of services designed to improve efficiency, enhance security, and future-proof your technology infrastructure.

In today’s rapidly evolving digital landscape, protecting your data and networks is more critical than ever. That’s where ATS comes in. We offer managed security services (MSSP), providing 24/7 monitoring and proactive defense against cyber threats. Our comprehensive cybersecurity services include everything from penetration testing and incident response to advanced data protection and network security. We focus on mitigating risks and preventing unauthorized access, ensuring that your critical assets are safe from emerging threats.

We understand the unique challenges businesses face when it comes to compliance and data security. As experts in cybersecurity compliance, we help companies navigate complex regulations and ensure that their systems are up to code. Our risk management services are designed to identify vulnerabilities and implement strategies to strengthen your defenses. Whether you’re looking to improve your data loss prevention (DLP) systems, implement multi-factor authentication (MFA), or upgrade your firewall management, we have the expertise to help.

For both businesses and homeowners, cloud security has become a priority as more services and data move to the cloud. At ATS, we offer secure cloud protection services that safeguard your data while providing seamless access across devices. We also specialize in endpoint protection, ensuring that all devices within your network are properly secured and free from threats like malware, ransomware, and phishing attacks. Our team also provides security awareness training, empowering employees and residents with the knowledge to recognize and prevent potential security threats.

One of the cornerstones of our cybersecurity approach is the implementation of a zero trust security model. This methodology ensures that every user, device, and connection is verified before being granted access to your network, offering an additional layer of protection. Combined with encryption services and data protection, our solutions create a secure, resilient environment for all your digital assets. Whether you need malware protection, cyber threat defense, or threat intelligence to stay ahead of cybercriminals, ATS provides you with the tools to defend against both internal and external risks.

As the demand for managed IT security services grows, Alberta Tech Solutions remains at the forefront of the cybersecurity industry in Edmonton. Our team is constantly refining its skill set and staying up-to-date with the latest trends in cybersecurity, ensuring that we can offer the most effective solutions to our clients. From penetration testing to security incident remediation, we work closely with you to understand your unique needs and deliver tailored security solutions that offer both immediate protection and long-term resilience.

At ATS, we are passionate about helping our clients build secure digital infrastructures that are both efficient and future-proof. Whether you’re a business looking for comprehensive IT security solutions or a homeowner interested in protecting your smart devices, we are here to help. Our holistic approach to cybersecurity ensures that every part of your digital world is protected—whether it’s your network, cloud data, or personal devices.

Contact Alberta Tech Solutions today to learn more about how our industry-leading cybersecurity solutions and IT security services can protect your data, enhance your productivity, and give you the peace of mind you deserve in an increasingly digital world.
