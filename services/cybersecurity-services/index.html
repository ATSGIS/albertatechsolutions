Cybersecurity Services for Business Customers

Comprehensive Protection for Your Business

Your business data, networks, and systems are vulnerable to numerous threats, and it’s crucial to implement multi-layered cybersecurity solutions to defend against potential breaches. ATS Edmonton specializes in providing businesses of all sizes with enterprise-level protection. From firewall management to penetration testing and incident response, our team is equipped to safeguard every aspect of your digital infrastructure.

Key Services for Businesses:

  • Managed Security Services (MSSP): Our 24/7 monitoring and incident response services ensure your systems are always protected against emerging threats.
  • Network Security: Protect your business network with advanced firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and multi-factor authentication (MFA).
  • Data Protection & Compliance: We provide data loss prevention (DLP) tools and encryption services to ensure your business complies with GDPR, HIPAA, and other industry regulations.
  • Penetration Testing: Our experts simulate real-world attacks to identify vulnerabilities before hackers can exploit them, enhancing your defenses.
  • Ransomware Protection: We implement backup solutions, advanced malware detection, and email security to safeguard your business from ransomware attacks.

Why ATS Edmonton for Your Business?

Your business deserves a cybersecurity partner that understands the complexities of protecting sensitive data while maintaining business continuity. ATS Edmonton offers tailored cybersecurity strategies, ensuring your digital infrastructure is fortified against cybercriminals. Our expert team will design a comprehensive defense plan based on your business’s unique needs, empowering you to focus on growth without worrying about security.

Protecting Your Home from Cyber Threats

As the world becomes more digitally connected, securing your home and personal data is more important than ever. ATS Edmonton provides residential clients with cybersecurity solutions designed to safeguard everything from smart home devices to your personal information stored on mobile devices and computers.

Key Services for Homeowners:

  • Smart Home Security: Protect your IoT devices, including smart thermostats, security cameras, and voice assistants, from cyberattacks.

  • Incident Response & Recovery: In the unfortunate event of a cyberattack, we provide data recovery and incident response services to mitigate damage and restore normalcy.

Cybersecurity Services for Elite Professionals and Individuals

Tailored Protection for Individuals & Professionals

As an elite professional or individual, you are at risk of specific threats due to the valuable information you handle. Whether you’re a CEO, entrepreneur, or someone with sensitive personal data, it’s crucial to have a cybersecurity strategy designed to protect your digital presence.

Key Services for Elite Professionals:

  • Data Encryption: Secure your confidential files, emails, and communications with robust encryption technologies, ensuring that only authorized parties can access them.
  • Advanced Endpoint Protection: Whether you’re using a laptop, smartphone, or tablet, ATS offers cutting-edge protection to keep your devices safe from malware, spyware, and cyberattacks.
  • Identity & Access Management (IAM): Manage and monitor who can access your sensitive information, ensuring that only trusted individuals can view or alter important documents.
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Add an extra layer of security to your accounts with MFA, reducing the risk of unauthorized access to your personal or professional data.
  • Phishing & Ransomware Protection: ATS offers proactive protection against phishing emails and ransomware attacks, ensuring that your online interactions remain secure.
