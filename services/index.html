Our Services

At Alberta Tech Solutions (ATS), we specialize in providing cybersecurity solutions, IT security services, and smart home automation tailored to your specific needs. We offer comprehensive managed security services (MSSP), including 24/7 network security, endpoint protection, firewall management, and incident response to ensure your systems are always secure. Our penetration testing and vulnerability assessments help identify and mitigate potential risks before they become threats. Whether you’re a small business or a large enterprise, we deliver data protection, cloud security, and data loss prevention (DLP) strategies that keep your digital assets safe from cyber-attacks and unauthorized access. We also provide cybersecurity compliance services, helping you meet regulatory requirements while securing your business.

In addition to IT security services, we offer innovative smart home automation solutions designed to enhance convenience, comfort, and security in your home. From smart lighting and thermostats to fully automated security systems, we integrate the latest technology to create a connected and secure living environment. Whether you’re looking to improve your home automation or upgrade your surveillance systems with advanced CCTV cameras and motion detectors, ATS provides customized solutions to meet your needs. Our team is committed to delivering scalable and reliable IT infrastructure that supports both business operations and modern living. With multi-factor authentication (MFA), encryption services, and cutting-edge cyber threat defense, ATS is your trusted partner in securing your digital world and future-proofing your technology infrastructure.

Why Cybersecurity is Crucial for You

Cybersecurity protects your business from data breaches, cyberattacks, and financial loss. As digital threats grow, ensuring the security of your systems, data, and customer information is essential for maintaining trust, continuity, and compliance. A strong cybersecurity strategy helps prevent downtime, safeguard sensitive data, and protect your reputation. Don't wait—secure your business today.
