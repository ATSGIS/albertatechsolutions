We Serve

At Alberta Tech Solutions (ATS), we specialize in providing comprehensive cybersecurity and smart tech solutions to businesses and homeowners across Edmonton and Western Canada. Our team of experts offers a wide range of services designed to protect your critical assets and enhance your digital environment. From Managed Security Services (MSSP) and data protection to smart home automation, we are your trusted partner in securing your systems and optimizing your home’s convenience, security, and energy efficiency.

We offer tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of both businesses and homeowners. Whether you’re looking to protect your company’s IT infrastructure with network security, cloud security, penetration testing, and endpoint protection, or enhance your home with smart home technology and advanced security systems, ATS has you covered.

Whether you’re a business seeking to protect sensitive data or a homeowner wanting to automate your space with smart technology, ATS delivers scalable, customized solutions to meet your needs.
